  • This is the first web platform designed as a one-stop hub specifically targeting medical students, residents, fellows, and attending physicians. Including informational resources as well as essentially a virtual bulletin board for research opportunities, Medsopotamia provides students the opportunity to learn about conducting research and connect with project leaders/mentors to increase publication output for everyone.
















  • Medsopotamia works by targeting the lack of connection of medical students and residents. This will be a means to increase younger student mentorship, research output for all students, and manifest a stronger sense of a “community of scholars”.
